# Product Explainer Article Template

Follow this structure when generating a Product Explainer article. Adapt section count and depth based on the product complexity and target word count.

---

## Structure

```
**Meta description:** [Under 160 characters. Include the primary keyword. Describe what the reader will learn.]

# [Article title — sentence case, includes keyword]

[Opening hook: 1–2 sentences. State the user problem this product solves or the opportunity it unlocks. Make it relatable.]

[Bridge paragraph: 1–2 sentences. Introduce the product as the solution. Mention Bybit by name.]

## Key takeaways

- [Takeaway 1 — the single most important thing]
- [Takeaway 2 — a practical benefit]
- [Takeaway 3 — a differentiator or unique feature]

## What is [Product Name]?

[2–3 paragraphs. Explain the product in plain language. Cover:]
- What it does
- Who it's for
- Why it matters (benefit, not feature)
- How it fits into the broader Bybit ecosystem

[If relevant, include a comparison or context paragraph — e.g., "Unlike traditional spot trading, [Product] lets you..."]

## How to [primary action] on Bybit

[Brief intro: 1 sentence setting up the tutorial. E.g., "Follow these steps to get started."]

### Step 1: [Action verb] + [what to do]

[1–2 paragraphs explaining this step. Be specific about what to click, where to navigate, what to enter.]

[Screenshot: description of what the UI shows at this step]

### Step 2: [Action verb] + [what to do]

[1–2 paragraphs]

[Screenshot: description]

### Step 3: [Action verb] + [what to do]

[1–2 paragraphs]

[Screenshot: description]

[Continue with as many steps as needed — typically 4–8 for a complete tutorial]

## Tips for [using/maximizing/getting the most from] [Product Name]

- [Tip 1 — practical, actionable]
- [Tip 2 — addresses a common mistake or misconception]
- [Tip 3 — an advanced move for experienced users]
- [Optional: Tip 4]

## [Optional: FAQ section — include if the product has common questions]

### [Question 1 — phrased as the user would ask it]

[Answer — 2–4 sentences. Direct and clear.]

### [Question 2]

[Answer]

### [Question 3]

[Answer]

## The bottom line

[1–2 paragraphs. Summarize the value proposition. Restate the key benefit. End with a forward-looking statement or encouragement.]

[CTA — clear call to action. E.g., "Ready to start? Head to [Product Page] on Bybit and try it today."]
```

---

## Guidelines

- **Word count**: 1,500–2,000 words (adjust based on product complexity)
- **Screenshots**: Minimum 3 for any tutorial. Place at the exact step they illustrate.
- **Step naming**: Always start with an action verb (Log in, Navigate, Select, Enter, Confirm, Review)
- **Jargon**: Define any crypto/trading term on first use if the target audience is new users
- **Links**: Reference Bybit pages naturally (e.g., "on the [Spot Trading](https://www.bybit.com/en/spot) page")
- **Key takeaways**: Always include this section after the intro — readers scan for it
- **FAQ**: Include if the product has 3+ commonly asked questions. Skip if not.
